<i>My Expenses</i> is your ultimate tool for effortless expense tracking and financial management, right at your fingertips!

Key Features:

    • <b>Effortless Expense Tracking</b>: Keep tabs on your expenses and income seamlessly, whether you're on your smartphone or tablet.
    • <b>Flexible Account Management</b>: Effortlessly manage multiple accounts, including transfers between different currencies.
    • <b>Streamlined Financial Planning</b>: Easily set up plans for recurring transactions.
    • <b>Seamless Data Management</b>: Export and import data with ease using QIF and CSV formats.
    • <b>Enhanced Security</b>: Protect your data with password or device lock screen security.
    • <b>Customizable Experience</b>: Tailor the app to your preferences with customizable themes and font sizes.
    • <b>Bank Statement Reconciliation</b>: Easily compare transaction status with your bank statements for accurate financial tracking.
    • <b>Quick Data Entry</b>: Enjoy convenient access with homescreen widgets and shortcuts.
    • <b>Powerful Data Analysis</b>: Filter your data based on various criteria and visualize distribution and historical trends with dynamic graphs.

Unlock Premium Features:

    • <i>Contrib Key</i>: Unlock unlimited accounts, recurring transaction plans, and more.
    • <i>Extended Key</i>: Enjoy cloud synchronization, automated backups, and sophisticated CSV imports.
    • <i>Professional Key</i>: Take your financial management to the next level with budgeting tools, receipt scanning, and email support.

<i>My Expenses</i> requires the following <u>permissions</u>:

    • <i>Calendar</i>: Plans for future and recurrent transactions are stored either in a special local calendar or in any other calendar you assign.
    • <i>Internet</i>: Anonymous usage tracking, Crash reports, Cloud synchronization.
    • <i>Accounts</i>: Synchronization across devices via cloud services.
